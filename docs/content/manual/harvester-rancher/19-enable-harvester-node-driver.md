---
title: 19-Enable Harvester Node Driver	
---
1. Open Cluster Management
1. Click `Drivers` page and navigate to `Node Drivers` tab
1. Search harvester
1. Check Harvester and click `Activate`

## Expected Results
1. Status displayed Activated
